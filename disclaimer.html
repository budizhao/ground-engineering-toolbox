Engineering limitations

The current tools are based on simplified assumptions and reduced-order representations of ground behaviour. They do not account for all relevant factors that may control real project performance, such as:

  • site-specific geological variability
  • groundwater conditions
  • construction effects
  • time-dependent behaviour
  • cyclic loading or thermal interference
  • project-specific boundary conditions and design code requirements

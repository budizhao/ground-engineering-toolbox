Assumptions and notes

  • Prototype implementation for soft clay and static loading.
  • Soil properties are assumed constant with depth in this version.
  • The integrated resistance is a screening-level indicator from pult(z) integration, not a full pile-head design solution.
  • This tool is for preliminary evaluation only and should not replace detailed design or code-based analysis.

Method notes

  • This is a screening-level preliminary design tool for vertical closed-loop BHE systems.
  • The tool estimates a feasible field size from thermal demand and simplified ground thermal properties.
  • The unit extraction rate is estimated from simplified scaling with thermal conductivity and volumetric heat capacity.
  • Detailed design should consider annual load profile, thermal interference, fluid temperatures, pipe configuration, groundwater effects, and site constraints.
